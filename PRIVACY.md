# Hinekora Privacy Policy

Hinekora collects limited anonymous telemetry to help find crashes and improve
the app. Telemetry is enabled by default during setup and can be disabled at any
time in Settings -> Privacy & Telemetry.

## Crash Reporting

Hinekora uses Sentry for crash and error reports. Reports can include the error
type, stack trace, operating system, app version, and breadcrumbs that help
identify what failed.

Hinekora does not intentionally send Path of Exile account names, Client.txt
contents, gameplay recordings, or full local file paths. The app redacts known
username and path patterns before sending crash data where possible.

## Usage Analytics

Hinekora uses Umami at `analytics.hinekora.app` for anonymous usage analytics.
Analytics can include page views and named feature events, such as setup or
recording workflow events.

Hinekora does not send gameplay recordings or Client.txt contents to Umami.
Usage analytics are used as aggregate product signals, not to inspect individual
gameplay activity.

## Local Data

Hinekora stores settings, profiles, clip metadata, and recording metadata on
your device. Recordings and clips remain local unless you choose to share them
outside the app.

## Changes

This policy may change as Hinekora evolves. Updates will be tracked in this
repository.
