**Improved:** Keep using Hinekora while edited videos save.

Video saves now continue in the background with clear progress and controls, so you can move around the app and return without interrupting the result.

- **Save progress:** Follow the percentage and estimated time remaining alongside a muted preview, with a compact status available in the sidebar.
- **Safe navigation:** Keep editing or refresh Hinekora while a video saves, then return through **View** without losing the active save or editor project.
- **Cancellation:** Cancel from the save view or sidebar after confirmation; unfinished files are removed without adding a Saved Edits entry.
- **Clear guidance:** Dismissible notices explain background saving, cancellation, and how new edits affect only the next video.
- **Long edits:** Save edited timelines up to 24 hours long.
